# AI Governance Framework

## Tujuan

Menetapkan tata kelola penggunaan AI dalam EIOS.

## Principles

Human accountability, lawful use, security, privacy, transparency, traceability, fairness, reliability, proportionality.

## Controls

Use-case approval, data classification, model assessment, prompt/output logging, human review, incident response, periodic reassessment.

## Standar & Regulasi Rujukan

| Sumber | Kontribusi |
|---|---|
| **ISO/IEC 42001:2023** | Kerangka AI Management System (kebijakan, peran, kontrol, continual improvement) |
| **NIST AI RMF 1.0** | Fungsi Govern, Map, Measure, Manage |
| **EU AI Act 2024/1689** | Rujukan risk-tiering (unacceptable/high/limited/minimal) |
| **SE Menkominfo No. 9/2023** | Etika AI Indonesia: inklusivitas, kemanusiaan, keamanan, transparansi, akuntabilitas |
| **UU No. 27/2022 (PDP)** | Perlindungan data pribadi dalam pelatihan/inferensi |
| **PER-5/MBU/09/2022** | Integrasi AI ke dalam manajemen risiko BUMN |

AI di EIOS bersifat mendukung; akuntabilitas tetap pada manusia dan organ korporasi.
