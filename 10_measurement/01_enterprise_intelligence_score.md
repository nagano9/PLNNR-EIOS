# Enterprise Intelligence Score

## Tujuan

Menetapkan indikator kesehatan kecerdasan organisasi.

## Dimensions

Knowledge coverage, quality, reuse, decision traceability, learning closure, AI adoption, governance compliance, search success.

## Index

Skor komposit 0–100. Bobot awal harus divalidasi melalui pilot.

## Bobot Awal (indikatif — kalibrasi saat pilot)

| Dimensi | Bobot |
|---|---|
| Decision traceability | 20% |
| Governance compliance | 15% |
| Quality | 15% |
| Knowledge coverage | 12% |
| Reuse | 12% |
| Learning closure | 10% |
| AI adoption (responsible) | 10% |
| Search success | 6% |

Skor dilaporkan triwulanan ke Governance Council dan menjadi salah satu masukan maturity assessment. Bobot menekankan traceability dan compliance sejalan prioritas GCG BUMN.
