# EIOS Maturity Assessment

## Tujuan

Menetapkan metode penilaian kematangan.

## Domains

Governance, architecture, data, knowledge, decision, AI, people, process, technology, measurement.

## Assessment Output

Score, evidence, gap, risk, recommended action, target maturity, owner, deadline.

## Skala Kematangan

Selaras `07_intelligence_capital/03_knowledge_maturity_model.md`: Level 1 Documented → 2 Structured → 3 Connected → 4 Intelligent → 5 Compounding. Penilaian dilakukan tahunan per domain, menghasilkan roadmap perbaikan yang diprioritaskan. Metode scoring dapat mengadopsi pendekatan evidence-based (mis. gaya CMMI L/P/F/N) untuk objektivitas.
