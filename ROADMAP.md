## Sekuensing, Owner & Horizon (indikatif)

Roadmap bersifat *outcome*, bukan jadwal pasti. Titik awal konkret ada pada **`15_codex/04_activation_plan_90day.md`** (Fase 1–2, pilot).

| Fase | Horizon indikatif | Prasyarat | Owner utama |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 Knowledge Foundation | 0–3 bln (pilot) | Owner ditunjuk | Knowledge Governance Owner |
| 2 Decision Foundation | 3–6 bln | Konten pilot terisi | EIOS PO + Domain Owner |
| 3 Enterprise Intelligence | 6–12 bln | Data terhubung (Level 3) | Data/AI Architect |
| 4 AI-Native Enterprise | 12–18 bln | AI governance berjalan | Technology Owner |
| 5 Corporate Digital Brain | 18 bln+ | Loop compounding terbukti | Sponsor/Council |

> Setiap kenaikan fase memerlukan bukti maturity (lihat `10_measurement/05`), bukan berlalunya waktu. Horizon dikalibrasi setelah pilot.
