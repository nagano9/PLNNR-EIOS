# Training and Capability Framework

## Tujuan

Menetapkan kompetensi yang diperlukan.

## Capabilities

Knowledge capture, metadata, ontology, decision framing, AI literacy, source verification, prompt design, risk awareness.

## Learning Path

Foundation → Practitioner → Steward → Architect → Executive.

## Modul Kunci

AI literacy & responsible use (mengacu **SE Menkominfo 9/2023**), source verification & anti-hallucination, klasifikasi data & **UU 27/2022 PDP**, decision framing, dan penggunaan template EIOS. Pelatihan menjadi bagian change management dan dinilai pada maturity assessment (dimensi People).
