# Intelligence Balance Sheet

## Tujuan

Menetapkan kerangka pelaporan aset kecerdasan.

## Assets

Knowledge objects, validated decisions, lessons, playbooks, patterns, ontologies, AI skills, reusable models.

## Liabilities

Outdated knowledge, undocumented decisions, conflicting sources, orphaned data, unvalidated AI outputs, single-person dependency.

## Equity

Net organizational intelligence after considering quality, coverage, reuse, and risk.
